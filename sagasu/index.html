What Sagasu is used for...
Sagasu is used for your "questionable" needs, which are...

List of commands: 

1. ADVICE - Sends the author some advice of life.
2. EMOJI - Sends the author a random emoji.
3. INVITE - Sends the author a bot invite for the bot.
4. JOKE - Sends the author a random joke from multiple sources (Adding more soon).
5. KPOP - Sends the author a picture and name of an kpop musician/singer.
6. MEME - Sends the author a random meme.
7. NEKO - Sends the author a picture of an neko.
8. PURGE - Clears the amount of messages that is provided in the sent channel.
9. RANDOM - Sends the author a random response.
10. SEARCH - Sends the author information about the search of character or anime.
11. TENOR - Sends the author a gif from the search term provided on tenor.com.
12. TOPIC - Sends the author a random topic.
13. WHOASKED - Sends the author a random response on who asked.
14. REDDIT - Sends the author a random post from selected subreddit.
15. UWU - Sends the author a gif from tenor which is for uwu.
16. PAT - Sends the author a gif of someone patting someone in anime.
17. HUG - Sends the author a gif of someone hugging someone in anime.
18. WAIFU - Sends the author a gif of a anime waifu.
19. CRY - Sends the author a gif of a anime person crying.
20. KISS - Sends the author a gif of someone kissing someone in anime.
21. SLAP - Sends the author a gif of someone slaping someone in anime.
22. SMUG - Sends the author a gif of a anime person having a smug face.
23. PUNCH - Sends the author a gif of someone punching someone in anime.
24. LIVE - Sends the author the current live player count of a chosen game.
25. VOTE - Sends the author a link to vote for the bot.
26. POKE - Sends the mentioned user a poke.
27. JOIN - Joins the author's channel to play upbeat radio.
28. LEAVE - Leaves the author's channel to play upbeat radio.
29. VOLUME - Sets the volume for the music stream (1 - 100).

You can also @ or mention the bot to get information of the help page or other information (prefix...).
