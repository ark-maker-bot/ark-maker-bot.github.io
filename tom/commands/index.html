PLAY [song_url | search_term | playlist_url]
Joins and plays a requested song from youtube.

AGAIN [song_id]
Puts a copy of a selected song to the back.

BACK [song_id]
Removes the selected song to the back of the playlist.

CLEAR
Clears the music queue.

INFORMATION
Shows information about the current song.

DISCONNECT
Leaves the current voice channel.

LYRICS
Shows the lyrics of the current song.

PAUSE
Pauses the music stream.

PREV
Gets the previous song and puts it to the end of queue.

QUEUE
Shows the current music queue for that server.

REMAINING
Shows the remaining time left on the music.

REMOVE [song_id | song_name]
Removes a song with the song title or id.

REPLAY
Replays the song currently playing once.

RESUME
Resumes the music stream.

SHUFFLE
Shuffle song playlist.

SKIP
Skips to the next song in the queue.

STOP
Stops the music stream.

SWAP [song_id_1] [song_id_2]
Swaps the selected song to another position.

VOLUME [1-100]
Changes the volume of the music stream.

SEEK [1-max seconds in video]
Skips to the selected point in the song.
